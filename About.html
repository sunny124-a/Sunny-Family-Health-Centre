About Us

Trusted healthcare in Vryburg — built on experience, guided by compassion

Your Trusted Family Doctor in Vryburg

Sunny Family Health Care Centre is a patient-centred general practice located at 74 Vry Street, Vryburg, North West. We provide comprehensive primary healthcare services to individuals and families across the community.

Led by an experienced Family Physician, our practice brings together years of clinical experience spanning multiple healthcare settings. From routine consultations and chronic disease management to minor procedures and wellness services, we are committed to delivering professional, compassionate care.

We believe that quality healthcare should be accessible to everyone. That's why we accept all major medical aids and welcome cash patients at affordable rates.
